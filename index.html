What is Inside the Guide?

Explore the exact blueprint used by master horologists to understand, customize, and build high-precision movements.

01 / Mechanical Principles

Understanding Movements

A comprehensive breakdown of mainsprings, escapements, balances, and gear trains. Understand how potential energy transforms into micro-precision timekeeping.

02 / Setup & Tools

Essential Tooling

Learn to build your watchmaking workspace. Get recommendations on loupes, tweezers, screwdrivers, movement holders, and specialized lubrication kits.

03 / Part Procurement

Sourcing Components

Navigating swiss-made movement suppliers and parts manufacturers. Learn to inspect tolerances and source dials, hands, cases, and balance springs.

04 / Practical Execution

Mastering Assembly

Step-by-step disassembly and assembly guides for standard hand-wound and automatic calibers, featuring oiling charts and timing adjustments.

Master Horologist & Author
The Expert Behind The Book

Dedicated to preserving the heritage of traditional swiss craftsmanship.

Jean-Louis Garrigue has spent over 35 years restoring antique clocks, designing bespoke complications, and mentoring apprentices in Switzerland's famous watchmaking valleys.

Seeing a growing disconnect between industrial manufacturing and the timeless art of manual adjustment, Jean-Louis decided to document his practical methods. This eBook acts as a comprehensive reference guide, helping watchmakers bypass years of trial-and-error by implementing time-tested watch assembly practices.
