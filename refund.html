Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: July 9, 2026

At Lumina Marketing Agency LLC, we strive to deliver the highest quality educational resources on watchmaking. Please review our specific refund policies regarding digital downloads below.

1. The Digital Nature of Our Products

Unlike physical merchandise which can be returned and restocked, digital publications (eBooks in PDF and ePub formats) are downloaded directly to your device and cannot be physically returned.

Standard Refund Rule: Once you click the digital download link sent to your email or access the file content, the sale is considered consumed and is non-refundable.

2. Refund Windows & Exceptions

We accommodate exceptions in the following specific circumstances:

  • Accidental Duplicate Purchases: If you accidentally purchased the eBook twice (evidenced by duplicate transaction codes within a 24-hour window), contact us within 14 days and we will issue a full refund for the duplicate transaction.
  • Unaccessed Links: If you have not clicked the secure download link generated for your email address, you may request a refund within 14 days of purchase. We will audit download metrics on our servers prior to approving the refund.
  • Technical Corruption: In the rare event that the files are corrupted or unreadable on modern platforms, and our support team is unable to resolve the format issue for you within 48 hours, we will issue a full refund.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request an eligible refund, please email our support portal:

  1. Send an email from the address used during the checkout process to refunds@horologyguide.com.
  2. State your full name and the unique transaction ID / receipt number received from the payment processor.
  3. Briefly detail the reason for your refund inquiry.

4. Processing Timeline

Once your request is approved:

  • We will immediately block access to the corresponding digital download links and terminate your licensee credentials.
  • The refund will be credited back to your original payment card (Visa, MasterCard, or American Express) within 5 to 7 business days, depending on your card issuer’s clearing periods.
